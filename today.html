homework assignment

due on 9/11/21 at 11:59PM

To consume:

Review pivot table functionality in Google Sheets here.

To do:

1. Answer questions about a dataset on refugees in Rochester using pivot tables. Use the videos on Blackboard in the 'Videos/resources' section as a resource.

  1. What percentage of refugees in this dataset arrived in 1998? Show your work.
  2. What is the percent change between refugees who arrived in 1998 and refugees who arrived in 2008? Show your work.
  3. Which year saw the most stateless refugees arriving to Rochester, NY? Create a pivot table that helps you answer this question, and rename the tab ‘data_q4’.
  4. What percentage of female refugees in this dataset were born in Bosnia And Herzegovina? Create a pivot table that helps you answer this question, and rename the tab ‘data_q5’.
  5. During which years did Rochester, NY get more than 100 refugees from Bosnia and Herzegovina?
  6. Short answer: Do some research on these years and what was going on in Bosnia And Herzegovina. Write two paragraphs about why these years may have produced many refugees. Please cite your sources; please do not cite Wikipedia as a source.
  7. List the countries that have a 50% or higher percentage of refugees coming in with ‘good’ ability in writing in English. Create a pivot table that helps you answer this question, and rename the tab ‘data_q8’.
  8. List the years where Rochester had 30 or more unique birth countries represented in its incoming refugee population. (Hint: You will need to use COUNTUNIQUE for this). Create a pivot table that helps you answer this question, and rename the tab ‘data_q9’.
  9. From which birth countries did widowed people arrive to Rochester, NY completely alone?

2. Find two public datasets that you think could be relevant for a central New York audience; at least one public dataset must be compiled by a government entity. Submit a link for each and a paragraph (a few sentences, at least) explaining why they are relevant and newsworthy. You should use MuckRock and FOIAMapper for inspiration on what government entities may have public datasets.
